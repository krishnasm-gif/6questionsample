Question 2 – Answer briefly (5 × 2 = 10 marks)

  1. Write two sentences describing how big Ashoka’s empire was.
    Show sample answer
    Ashoka’s empire covered almost the whole of India except the far south. It stretched from Afghanistan in the west to Odisha in the east and from the Himalayas to northern Karnataka.
  2. Name two great things Samudragupta achieved as a ruler.
    Show sample answer
    1. He defeated many kings in north and south India.
    2. He performed the Ashvamedha sacrifice to show his power.
  3. What do we mean by “wards” in a Nagar Panchayat?
    Show sample answer
    Wards are small areas or divisions in a town from which people elect one councilor each to the Nagar Panchayat.
  4. Mention any two important discoveries or ideas of Aryabhata.
    Show sample answer
    1. He said the Earth rotates on its axis.
    2. He worked on the value of pi (π) and the place value system.
  5. According to the 74th Constitutional Amendment, what is a metropolitan area?
    Show sample answer
    A metropolitan area is a big urban region with more than 10 lakh people, including the main city and nearby towns or outgrowths.

Question 3 – Explain the following terms (any 10) (1 mark each)

  1. Janapada
    Show answer
    Early tribal areas or small states in ancient India that later grew into bigger kingdoms.
  2. Dhamma
    Show answer
    Ashoka’s policy of kindness, truth, non-violence and respect for all religions.
  3. Monarchy
    Show answer
    A type of government ruled by a king or queen, usually passed from parent to child.
  4. Hellenic
    Show answer
    Style or influence coming from ancient Greek culture, especially seen in Gandhara art.
  5. Octroi
    Show answer
    Tax charged by a municipality on goods brought into a city.
  6. Gandhara Art
    Show answer
    Buddhist art style from north-west India that mixed Indian and Greek features.
  7. Municipal Corporation
    Show answer
    Local government body for very big cities that manages water, roads, garbage, etc.
  8. Mahamatras
    Show answer
    Special officers appointed by Ashoka to spread dhamma and check administration.
  9. Republic
    Show answer
    A state ruled by elected leaders or a council instead of a king.
  10. Brahmi
    Show answer
    Ancient script used for Ashoka’s rock and pillar edicts.
